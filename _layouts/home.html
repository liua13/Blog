ABOUT ME

I'm a first year undergraduate student at MIT studying computer science with a minor in creative writing. Coding and writing, in my eyes, are extremely similar: Through the lines of code I write, I’m communicating with computers the same way I communicate with the people around me. The only difference is, instead of English, I’m using Python (or Java, etc). My interests lie in understanding people and using the tools I have to solve problems we face, whether it's through books or technology. Aside from coding and writing, I enjoy reading, running, listening to music, playing card / board games, and spending time with friends.
